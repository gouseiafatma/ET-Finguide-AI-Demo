# 💰 ET FinGuide AI

🚀 AI Financial Decision Engine (ET Hackathon)

---

## 🧩 Problem

Most people struggle with managing money.  
They don’t know:

- How much to save  
- Where to invest  
- How to plan monthly finances  

---

## 💡 Solution

ET FinGuide AI helps users:

- Understand financial health  
- Get smart financial decisions  
- Follow a monthly action plan  
- Adjust plans based on life events  

---

## 🧠 Features

### 1. Financial Health Score
- Score out of 100
- Detects overspending & low savings

### 2. Smart Decisions
- SIP recommendations  
- Expense reduction tips  
- Insurance suggestions  

### 3. Monthly Plan
- Week-wise financial actions  

### 4. Life Event Simulator 🚀
- Bonus / Salary increase / Expense  
- AI updates plan dynamically  

---

## 📱 Demo Flow

1. Enter financial details  
2. View financial score  
3. Get smart recommendations  
4. Follow monthly plan  
5. Simulate life event → updated plan  

---

## ⚙️ Tech Stack

- Frontend: HTML, CSS  
- Backend: Flask (Prototype level)  
- Logic: Rule-based AI
